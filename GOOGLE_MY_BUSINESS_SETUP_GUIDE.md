## üöÄ Step-by-Step Setup Process

### Step 1: Create Google Business Profile

1. **Go to Google Business Profile**
   - Visit: https://business.google.com
   - Click "Manage now" or "Get started"

2. **Sign in with Google Account**
   - Use your business Gmail account
   - If you don't have one, create a dedicated business Gmail

3. **Enter Business Name**
   - Type: "Prayan Masale"
   - If it appears in suggestions, select it
   - If not, click "Create a business with this name"

### Step 2: Choose Business Category

**Primary Category Options:**
- "Spice store" (Best option)
- "Food & beverage company"
- "Organic food store"
- "Specialty food store"

**Additional Categories to Add:**
- "Organic food store"
- "Health food store"
- "Online retailer"
- "Food manufacturer"

### Step 3: Business Location Setup

**Option A: Physical Storefront**
- Enter complete address
- Set service area radius
- Mark on map accurately

**Option B: Online-Only Business**
- Select "I deliver goods and services to my customers"
- Choose service areas (cities/regions you deliver to)
- Hide address if no physical storefront

### Step 4: Contact Information

**Phone Number:**
- Use your primary business number
- Ensure it's answered during business hours
- Consider WhatsApp Business number

**Website:**
- Primary: https://prayanmasale.com
- Additional: Social media links

### Step 5: Verification Process

**Verification Methods:**
1. **Phone Verification** (Fastest)
   - Google calls your business number
   - Enter the verification code

2. **Postcard Verification** (If physical address)
   - Google sends postcard to business address
   - Takes 5-7 business days
   - Enter code from postcard

3. **Email Verification** (Sometimes available)
   - Verification link sent to business email
