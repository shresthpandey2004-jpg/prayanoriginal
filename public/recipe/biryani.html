Instructions

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs
  3. Cook rice with whole spices until 70% done
  4. Cook marinated chicken separately
  5. Layer rice and chicken alternately
  6. Cook on dum (slow cooking) for 45 minutes
  7. Serve hot with raita and shorba
